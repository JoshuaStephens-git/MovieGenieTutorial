Behind the Magic

Your day is done, dinner is ready, and it’s finally time to relax. You’re determined to watch something new and exciting. 30 minutes and a cold meal later, you reluctantly put on the same movie you’ve seen a dozen times.

Sound familiar? I created The Movie Genie because I was tired of the cold dinner and endless scroll.

My name is Josh, and this website started as a way for me to learn the basics of Spring Boot and Thymeleaf. I wanted to create a simple, easy-to-use movie recommendation tool that didn't take a bunch of configuration to get a movie suggestion. Once I (mostly) completed the initial project, I decided it would be a great opportunity to create an in-depth tutorial on how to replicate this project. The original, found here, showcases the complete project. I hope you find this website and tutorial useful in developing your own The Movie Genie!

The Genie's Next Wish

The magic is just getting started! In the future, I plan to incorporate even more features:

  • More Filters: Narrow down choices by mood, streaming service, and more.
  • TV Genie: Find the perfect TV show as easily as you find a movie.
  • Personal Watchlist: Save your favorite movie picks directly in your browser to view later.

The Movie Genie is a work in progress, so your feedback is incredibly valuable. If you have a suggestion or find a bug, please send it my way via the contact page.

Thanks for visiting, and I hope the Genie makes your next movie night a great one!
